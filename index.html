A church, church building or church house is a building used for Christian worship services and other Christian religious activities. The earliest identified Christian church building is a house church founded between 233 and 256.



School Library

was established in 1980

A school library is a library within a school where students, staff, and often, parents of a public or private school have access to a variety of resources.

was established in 1982

An auditorium is a room built to enable an audience to hear and watch performances. For movie theatres, the number of auditoria is expressed as the number of screens.
